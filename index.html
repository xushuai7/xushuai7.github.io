I am a lecturer at College of Computer Science and Technology, NUAA. I am a professional member of ACM, IEEE and CCF. Besides, I serve as the academic secretary of CCF YOCSEF Nanjing from 2021 to 2022. I received my B.E. degree in computer science from Northeastern University in 2014, after which I obtained my Ph.D. degree in computer science from Southeast University in 2020, under supervision of Professor Jiuxin Cao. I used to be a CSC Joint-PhD student (from 2018.11 to 2020.05) at Georg-August-University of Goettingen, Germany, under supervision of Professor Xiaoming Fu. From 2020.12, I work as a lecturer at NUAA.

My research focuses on:
  • Smart City: traffic flow prediction, air quality prediction.
  • Social Media Data Mining: user mobility modeling and prediction, link prediction, influence maximization.
  • Location-based Social Network: point-of-interest recommendation, venue rating prediction.
