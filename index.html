I am a tenured associate professor at College of Computer Science and Technology, NUAA. I am a professional member of ACM/IEEE/CCF/CAAI. I received my B.E. degree in computer science from Northeastern University in 2014, after which I obtained my Ph.D. degree in computer science from Southeast University in 2020, under supervision of Professor Jiuxin Cao. I used to be a CSC Joint-PhD student (from 2018.11 to 2020.05) at Georg-August-University of Goettingen, Germany, under supervision of Professor Xiaoming Fu. I joined NUAA at December 2020.

My research focuses on:

  • Smart City: human mobility prediction, traffic flow prediction, air quality forecasting.
  • Social Media Data Mining: user behavior modeling, link prediction, dis-information detection.
  • Location-based Social Network: point-of-interest recommendation.
